Privacy Policy for Spirit Journal

Effective date: April 19, 2026

Spirit Journal respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

Spirit Journal is designed to store your journal entries locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information to the developer or third parties.

Journal Content

Journal entries you create, edit, search, sort, and delete are stored locally on your device using the app’s local database. This information is not sent to our servers.

Backup and Restore

Spirit Journal allows you to create and restore backups in JSON format. These backups are created only when you choose to export them and are stored in a location you select. Restoring from a backup only occurs when you choose a backup file to import.

Data Sharing

Spirit Journal does not share your personal data with third parties.

Advertising

Spirit Journal does not use advertising services and does not use the Advertising ID.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

Spirit Journal does not use third-party analytics or crash reporting services.

Data Security

Because Spirit Journal stores data locally on your device, you are responsible for protecting access to your device and any backup files you export.

Your Choices

You can edit or delete journal entries at any time within the app. You can also delete backup files that you created. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored app data, subject to your device and Android system behavior.

Children’s Privacy

Spirit Journal is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted at the same location where this policy is published.
